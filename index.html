Popular Games

Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a traditional contact sport originating from ancient India. The sport is particularly popular in the Indian subcontinent, including countries like India, Bangladesh, Nepal, and Pakistan.

Cricket

Cricket is believed to have been invented in England during the late 16th century. The earliest definite reference to the sport is from a court case in 1597, and by the 17th century, it was well-established in the southeastern counties of England.

Football

Football, also known as soccer, is the world's most popular sport, played by millions across the globe. It originated from various ancient ball games and was codified in England in the 19th century with the establishment of the Football Association.
